Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 1, 2026

Plain Language Summary

We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information.
We do not track you, serve ads, or share data with third parties.
Market data is fetched from public sources and displayed — never stored on our servers.
Your settings stay on your device only (Apple's UserDefaults, sandboxed to the app).
Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

1. Who We Are

GoldVision ("the App", "we", "our") is an iOS application developed and maintained by Ghassan Jaam. The App is published on the Apple App Store under the bundle identifier com.ghassanjaam.goldvision.

For privacy questions, contact us at axesalon@hotmail.ca.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

GoldVision does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, serial number)
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Financial data, trading accounts, or brokerage credentials
  • Camera, microphone, contacts, or photos
  • Payment or billing information (handled exclusively by Apple)
  • Crash reports or diagnostics (no third-party crash SDK is integrated)

3. Data Stored on Your Device Only

The following information is stored locally on your device using Apple's sandboxed storage and never leaves it:

  • App preferences — selected timeframe, onboarding completion status (UserDefaults)
  • Price alerts — thresholds you configure in the Alerts screen (UserDefaults)
  • Paper trading history — virtual trade records and P&L (local file storage)
  • Trade journal entries — notes and trade logs you write (local file storage)
  • Signal history — past buy/sell signal log for your reference (local file storage)

None of this data is backed up to our servers, transmitted over the network, or shared with any party. It lives exclusively in the app's sandboxed container on your device.

4. Market Data & Network Requests

To display live and historical gold prices, GoldVision makes outbound HTTPS requests to publicly available financial data services:

  • Yahoo Finance — live XAU/USD spot price and historical candlestick data (query1.finance.yahoo.com)
  • Public RSS feeds — financial news headlines from sources such as Reuters and MarketWatch

These requests are one-way data pulls — no user data is included in the request payload. Your IP address may be visible to these services as part of normal HTTPS connectivity, the same as any web browser visit. GoldVision does not control those services' privacy practices.

5. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases APPLE IAP

GoldVision Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store in-app purchase system (StoreKit). We never see, store, or process your payment card details.

Apple provides us with an anonymised receipt indicating whether a subscription is active. No personally identifiable information is shared with us as part of this process. Apple's privacy policy governs all payment handling: apple.com/legal/privacy.

To manage, cancel, or view your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, GoldVision delivers local push notifications for price alerts and high-impact economic events. These notifications are generated entirely on-device by iOS — no notification payload is routed through our servers. We do not use Apple Push Notification service (APNs) or any third-party push service.

You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications → GoldVision.

7. Children's Privacy

GoldVision is intended for users aged 17 and older, consistent with its financial information content. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the App, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

GoldVision contains no third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking libraries. There are no embedded services from Google, Facebook, Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Sentry, Crashlytics, or equivalent providers.

The App's only external dependencies are Apple's native system frameworks (StoreKit, UserNotifications, WebKit, Charts) — all governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Because no data is collected or stored on our end, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf. All locally stored data (alerts, journal entries, signal history) can be removed at any time by:

  • Deleting GoldVision from your device (all app data is permanently erased by iOS)
  • Using the "Reset Disclaimer & Onboarding" option in Settings to clear preferences

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
